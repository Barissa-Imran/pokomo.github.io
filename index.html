Welcome to Pokomo Dictionary

Pokomo also known as Kipokomo or Kipfokomo is a language spoken in Tana River in Kenya by the Pokomo people.

Pokomo language has approximately 120,000 native speakers.

The language has 6 dialects; Gwano, Kinakomba, Malalulu, Ndera, Ndura and Zubaki.
